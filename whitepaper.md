# 1. Vision
As quantum computing technology advances, traditional cryptographic methods used in current blockchain infrastructures face unprecedented risks. LATTICE (LAT) is born from the necessity to explore and implement "Post-Quantum Cryptography" (PQC) within the Base ecosystem. Our mission is to build a resilient community that prioritizes long-term security against future decryption threats.
